KIDTECH GLOBAL ENTERPRISES

ABOUT US

Kidstech Global is a contemporary organization using modern educational support tools to facilitate learning process among the children in both primary and secondary schools relying on robotics, chess and scrabble games couple with modern artistic asset to influence the quantitative, qualitative reasoning with healthy creativity among the children to be able to compete with their counterpart in the rest of the world.

STRATEGY

We have well trained personnel that carry out responsibilities of coaching kids to attain the desired goal at different levels. Our target is to bring out the best from each kid we have an encounter with and the strategy is to pay sound attention to a child to make a conclusive deduction of his/her area of core competence and weakness in other to strengthen the strength and weaken the weakness of a child.
